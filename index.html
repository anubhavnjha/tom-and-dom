The Story So Far

Hey, I'm Anubhav Jha. If you're looking for someone who fits neatly into a single box, you might be in the wrong place. My life is a fast-paced balancing act between the roar of the cricket stadium, the busy lecture halls of Delhi University, and the quiet rhythm of a pen hitting paper.

Here is a look at the different channels I'm constantly tuning into:

  • On the Pitch: As a Division-19 cricketer, the boundary ropes are where I test my limits. Cricket teaches me how to handle high-pressure situations, think three steps ahead, and show up every single day ready to serve.
  • In the Classroom: When I don't have a bat in hand, I'm navigating the campus paths at DU. Being a university student is all about expanding my worldview, asking tough questions, and laying the groundwork for what comes next.
  • Between the Lines: Beyond the sports gear and the textbooks, I lose myself in poetry and writing. It's where I slow down the chaos of daily life, capture raw emotions, and make sense of the world through rhythm and rhyme.

My Conclusion

I'm just a guy chasing a massive dream, trying to perfect my cover drive by day and my stanzas by night. So don't just stand on the boundary line—take a look around, explore my latest projects, or drop a message if you want to collaborate on something game-changing. Let's build something unforgettable.

To My Teacher, My Guide: Sir HV

To my high school English teacher, Mr. Harshvardhan Roy—whom, with the utmost respect, I will always call Sir HV.

Sir HV didn't just teach me from a syllabus; he unlocked my love of literature, English, and the art of storytelling, sparking an interest in writing and poetry that changed the trajectory of my life. But his lessons extended far beyond the walls of the classroom—he taught me how to live. To me, he was much more than a teacher; he was a profound father figure whose wisdom and warmth shaped who I am today.

Some of my fondest memories are of our long conversations and intense debates. We would dive into everything—from the intricacies of English literature and the beauty of different languages to the complexities of politics and the deeper meaning of life itself. He challenged my thinking, broadened my worldview, and taught me how to articulate my thoughts with conviction.

To My Tom:

Alongside the profound foundation laid by Sir HV, a massive share of the credit goes to Tom. Tom has been the ultimate inspiration behind my creative expression—the very person for whom I have written a great number of my poems, and for whom I will undoubtedly continue to write for years to come. If Sir HV taught me how to live, it was Tom who gave those words a target and a purpose.

It is because of the belief, the debates, and the deep inspiration from both of them that I began this poetic journey. Thank you for the guidance, the arguments, the poetry, and the lifelong lessons.
